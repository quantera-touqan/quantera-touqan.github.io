Annual Meeting for QuantERA Project TouQan in Cambridge

Posted on Tue 04 March 2025

We had our first annual meeting of the TouQan project, which took place from February 10–15, 2025, at the University of Cambridge. During the meeting our various partners discussed the progress of the project together and ongoing collaborations! We also had a 1-afternoon workshop open to the Cambridge community with talks by our PIs, see here for abstracts of the talks!

List of Participants

The following members of the collaboration attended:

  • Instituto de Física Teórica CSIC - Madrid: Alvaro Alhambra, Matteo Scandi
  • University of Cambridge, UK: Angela Capel Cuevas
  • University of Tübingen: Tim Möbus
  • Inria, École Normale Supérieure de Lyon, France: Mischa Woods
  • University of Copenhagen: Daniel Stilck França
  • Center for Theoretical Physics PAS, Warsaw: Michał Oszmaniec
  • Hamburg University of Technology, Germany: Martin Kliesch, Özgün Kum
